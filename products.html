Our products

All our chocolates are made by hand by our trained chocolatiers. In addition to the products listed below, we also take custom orders.

  • Chocolates

    Our unctuous chocolates are made fresh daily in small batches, and come in a wide range of flavours, including caramel, orange cream, mint, and espresso. Each chocolate is hand-crafted and made with free-trade cocoa.

  • Candy

    Choose your favourites among our 28 varieties of colourful candy, including jujubes, gobstoppers, lollipops, lemon drops, and jelly beans. All our candy is made with organic ingredients and contains no artificial flavours or colours.
