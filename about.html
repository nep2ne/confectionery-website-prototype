About

Established in 1892, Coco's Chocolatiers is a family-owned business located in the heart of Manotick, Ontario. Our chocolatiers have been trained in the traditional methods passed down by generations of confectioners, and have also attended renowned European cooking schools. We take pride in our craftsmanship and our personal touch.
